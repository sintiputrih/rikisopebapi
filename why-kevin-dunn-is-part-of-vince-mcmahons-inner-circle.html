Why Kevin Dunn Is Part Of Vince McMahons Inner Circle

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, April 9, 2024

Kevin Dunn is the Executive Producer & Chief, Global Television Distribution in WWE. Dunn is also infamous for many fans due to the numerous camera cuts for WWE’s product, which fans simply can’t stand. It seems the real reason for Dunn being part of Vince McMahon’s circle has been revealed.

Kevin Dunn is also best-known thanks to Jim Cornette’s rants where he called him “Bucky Beaver.” In fact, Kevin Dunn was also said to be like god backstage in WWE by Chelsea Green.

For several years, a rumor regarding Kevin Dunn has been making rounds. As the rumors state, Dunne’s father had saved WWF tapes from a burning trailer. Vince McMahon was said to have felt so indebted to Dunne and his father that he guaranteed them jobs for life.

While speaking Wrestling Observer Radio, Dave Meltzer said that he had heard of the story before. However, it seems that is not why Kevin Dunn is part of Vince McMahon’s inner circle. The rumor is why Dunn got a job in WWE, but the reason why Dunn got into McMahon’s inner circle is that he is someone that “Vince can deal with.”

Kevin Dunn doesn’t have a big fanbase and there are multiple reasons for that. It was also said that Kevin Dunn kept Jessica McKay down in WWE. Regardless, Dunn will continue to be a part of WWE for many years to come and that is unlikely to change anytime soon.
