Who is the Girlfriend of Von Miller? His Parents, Family, Jersey, Salary

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 30, 2024

Von Miller plays outside linebacker in American football for the National Football League’s Buffalo Bills. Miller played college football for Texas A&M, where he was named a unanimous All-American and won the Butkus Award for being the best linebacker in the country. In the 2011 NFL Draft, the Denver Broncos selected him with the second overall pick.

Miller has been selected for eight Pro Bowls and has been named to the first team of the All-Pro team three times and the second team four times. Miller received the Super Bowl MVP award following the Broncos’ victory against the Carolina Panthers in Super Bowl 50, which marked the end of the 2015 NFL season. Miller was acquired by the Los Angeles Rams in a trade in 2021. Miller won his second Super Bowl with the Rams when they defeated the Bengals in Super Bowl LVI.

Von Miller Girlfriend

Von is a star player at the Bills and is not married or engaged to anyone yet. But it’s not like he was not engaged earlier. Von had many relationships and affairs earlier. He was spotted with the model and actor, Elizabeth Ruiz, earlier, but they broke up.

Then he was spotted with Lira Galore, another model, but it was not confirmed whether they were dating or not, but they do posts pictures of each other on their social media handle.

After that, in 2018, Von started dating a stripper named Megan Denise. Megan was a stripper in Las Vegas and came into the limelight when she was spotted with the Baller. They got engaged after dating for two years, but things messed up between them and they parted ways. But before their breakup, Denise was pregnant with his child.

Megan then contacted Von but the message leaked of their private conversation, where Von said it was a huge mistake and prayed for the miscarriage of the child. So it became a huge controversy at that time.
