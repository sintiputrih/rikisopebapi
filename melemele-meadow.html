Melemele Meadow (Japanese: メレメレの花園 Melemele Flower Garden) is a location on Melemele Island in Alola, located west of Route 3. A hidden tunnel in the west of the first flower field leads to Seaward Cave.

Geography

Melemele Meadow is a large field of yellow flowers, with several elevated platforms of stone running around its edges and through its center. It is the source of the Yellow Nectar in Alola and popular among tourists. Its southwest corner houses a small secret entrance to Seaward Cave, which the player must visit during their first visit to the meadow in order to find Nebby. A Poké Finder photo spot is located on the highest cliff overlooking the meadow.

In the anime

Melemele Meadow in the anime

Melemele Meadow appeared in A Mission of Ultra Urgency!, where a Buzzwole appeared there through an Ultra Wormhole during the filming of an episode of Alolan Detective Laki. After Ash and his classmates had succeeded in catching said Buzzwole at Mahalo Trail, they returned to the meadow, allowing Buzzwole to be sent back home through another wormhole temporarily opened by Professor Burnet.

In the manga

Pokémon Adventures

Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun & Ultra Moon arc

Melemele Meadow appeared in a flashback in Homecoming and the Brilliant Professional Golfer. Kahili, after returning to Alola at the request of Hala, decided to investigate Melemele Island. Sometime after arriving in Melemele Meadow, Kahili was attacked by a Kartana and a Buzzwole. She attempted to fight the two Ultra Beasts off, but they disappeared soon after.

In Flash and Cosmog's Secret, Professor Burnet mentioned that she has visited Melemele Meadow several times to gather Yellow Nectar.
