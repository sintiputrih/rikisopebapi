Kim Kardashian's Orbit Baby Stroller for Baby North Cost $940

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, April 7, 2024

Don't call it a stroller. It's a travel system. Of course, Baby North West wouldn't have any ol' pram for her first ride! A svelte Kim Kardashian was photographed on Wednesday, Oct. 9, pushing her 4-month-old daughter in an Orbit Baby G2 Travel System that costs $940. The two were heading into the Jimmy Kimmel Live studio to support love Kanye West, who was on the show to clear the air between him and the talk show host.

While the rapper's epic and long interview certainly made headlines, some parents-to-be may have been paying more attention to the couple's choice of stroller. According to a press release sent by Orbit Baby soon after pictures of North in her stroller surfaced, the deluxe "travel system is perfect for North's jet-setting parents because the state-of-the-art car seat transitions seamlessly from the stroller base to the car."

Their on-the-go lifestyle may have been a factor in choosing the Orbit Baby stroller, but Kim's big sister Kourtney was likely the main reason. Kourtney has been using the same stroller since Mason, now 3, was born. Other celebrity Orbit Baby fans include Lisa Ling, Megan Fox, Vanessa Minnillo, and Malin Akerman.

Nori has been pampered to the extreme by the couple since her birth. Last week, proud mom Kim Instagrammed all the expensive swag the little girl received from fashion designers during their trip to Paris. North's haul included a Lanvin baby tee that cost $395 and a Lanvin black ruffle dress that cost $1,250.
