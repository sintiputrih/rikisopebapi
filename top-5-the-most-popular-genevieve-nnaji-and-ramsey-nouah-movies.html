TOP 5: The Most Popular Genevieve Nnaji and Ramsey Nouah Movies

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Whenever these two powerhouses are combined in a movie, we knew the result will be something that blows the mind.

Nollywood stars, Genevieve Nnaji and Ramsey Nouah are two of Nigeria’s biggest stars.

Today, we go back in time to see some top 5 movies these two starred in. Buckle up!

1. Church Business (2003)

Genevieve stars as Chioma, wife of Jimmy who is played by Ramsey Nouah. Jimmy’s family is plunged into abject poverty. As a result, he branches out into church ministry after a recommendation from a friend. Chioma shows she is not happy about this and later events show cause for why she was right to be worried. The movie was produced by Simi Opeoluwa.

2. Passion and Pain (2003)

A few things are stronger than true, deep desire. Is money one of them? Maureen, played by Genevieve Nnaji learns a lesson on love and life when she allows herself to be swayed by campus bad boy, Francis (Ramsey Nouah). Whether she chooses her old love Damian (Emeka Ike) when the odds turn against her is a statement that makes for radical conversation. It is directed by Tarila Thompson.

3. Weekend Getaway (2012)

Desmond Elliot brings this star-studded 2012 romantic drama to life, starring Genevieve Nnaji and Ramsey Nouah. The story follows a group of friends with different backstories, all of whom are looking to revive the spark in their respective relationships. The Le Meridian Hotel plays host to their weekend getaway.

4. Above Death: In God We Trust (2003)

A boy grows up to target his family and causes strange events to happen. Before he does this, we learn that his mother was once raped by an evil entity. It is directed by Simi Opeoluwa.

5. Camouflage (1999)

This 1999 Nollywood classic introduces a young girl who convinces her boyfriend that she is the definition of purity. Soon though, we discover the truth is so much more than meets the eye. Will she hold on to her camouflage longer than we expect?
