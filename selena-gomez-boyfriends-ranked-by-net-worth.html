Selena Gomez's Famous Ex Boyfriends Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, February 8, 2024

Highlights

  • Selena Gomez has had a string of relationships with rich and famous men, including Taylor Lautner, Niall Horan and The Weeknd.
  • These men have amassed impressive fortunes of their own, with net worths ranging from $40 million to $300 million.
  • Despite rumors and speculation, Justin Bieber is happily married to Hailey Bieber and is worth $300 million, while The Weeknd has a net worth of the same amount due to his successful music career.

When Selena Gomez was extremely young, she developed an interest in entertaining while watching her mom prepare for stage productions. Wanting to encourage their daughter, Gomez’s parents allowed her to audition for various roles. As a result, Selena got her first brush with fame when she was cast in the family show Barney & Friends.

Since Gomez got her first big break, she has never looked back. In fact, Gomez has enjoyed so much success as an actor and singer over the years that she is now worth $800 million. Given her drive to succeed and her incredible fortune, it isn’t surprising that Gomez has been involved with some rich and famous men.

6 How Much Is Taylor Lautner Worth?

Net Worth: $40 Million

  • Year(s) Dated: Briefly 2009

Between 2008 and 2010, Selena Gomez had an on-again-off-again relationship with someone who appears later in this list. During that timeframe, Gomez dated Taylor Lautner for a few months in 2009. Their quick romance left fans wondering what really happened between Gomez and Lautner.

Best known for his role in the Twilight movies, Lautner played a major role in all five films in that franchise enjoying a great deal of success. On top of his most famous role, Lautner has also played notable roles in shows like Scream Queens and movies like Valentine's Day, Grown Ups 2, and The Ridiculous 6 among others.

These days, Lautner is living a quieter life with his wife, Taylor, to whom he has been married since 2022.

Thanks to everything he has accomplished over the years (and after Twilight), Lautner has a $40 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

5 How Much Is Zedd Worth?

Net Worth: $50 Million

  • Year(s) Dated: January 2015 to April 2015

Another who was only involved with Gomez briefly is Zedd. Zedd dated the pop music princess from January to April 2015. Of all the people included in this list, Zedd is the least famous in North America by far.

A Russian-German DJ and music producer, Zedd rose to fame in 2012 after the release of his song Clarity. Since Zedd’s first brush with fame, he has released two albums and several hit songs, including Stay, The Middle, Break Free, and I Want You To Know with the last song featuring vocals from Gomez.

Extremely successful, Zedd has managed to accumulate a truly impressive $50 million fortune.

4 How Much Is Niall Horan Worth?

Net Worth: $70 Million

  • Year(s) Dated: Briefly 2015

As it turns out, 2015 was a massive year in Niall Horan'slife since around the same time One Direction split up, he dated Gomez for a month or two.

As one of the members of One Direction, Horan was part of one of the most successful boy bands of all time thanks to songs like What Makes You Beautiful, Story of My Life, and Perfect.

Since One Direction split up, Horan has released several popular songs that charted in multiple countries and he has enjoyed a great deal of success. He has also become a successful coach on The Voice, having a win under his belt for Season 23 and looking for another win for Season 24.

As a result of both eras of his career, Horan has earned a $70 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

3 How Much Is Nick Jonas Worth?

Net Worth: $70 Million

  • Year(s) Dated: 2008 to 2010

From one boy band member to another, Nick Jonas is the aforementioned person who dated Gomez off and on from 2008 until 2010.

Best known as a member of The Jonas Brothers alongside his siblings Kevin Jonas and Joe Jonas, the three of them seem to love making music together these days. With songs like Sucker, Burnin’ Up, What A Man Gotta Do, and Cool together, not to mention, The Album which they are promoting while on tour during The Tour, Nick has made a great deal of his fortune with his brothers.

Also an accomplished solo artist, Nick has released hit songs like Jealous and Close. Considering that his career has been very similar to Niall Horan’s, it is not surprising that Nick Jonas is also worth $70 million.

2 How Much Is Justin Bieber Worth?

Net Worth: $300 Million

  • Year(s) Dated: 2010 to 2018

Of all the people that Gomez has dated over the years, there is no doubt that her relationship with Justin Bieber has been talked about the most.

Together off and on from 2010 until 2018, Gomez and Bieber’s time together inspired many of the most popular songs that both of them have released.

Easily among the biggest pop stars of his generation, it has often seemed like Bieber has the Midas touch since every song he touches turns to gold or platinum. For example, Bieber’s songs Sorry, Baby, Love Yourself, Where Are Ü Now and Boyfriend were all massive hits. He also was featured in the re-release of Despacito.

Bieber has been married to his wife, Hailey Bieber (nee Baldwin) since 2018. Their whirlwind romance was such that they started dating in July and were married in September. Despite rumors that always swirl about Bieber being happier with Gomez and wanting out of his marriage, it does appear that Bieber and Baldwin are still very happy together.

1 How Much Is The Weeknd Worth?

Net Worth: $300 Million

  • Year(s) Dated: December 2916 to October 2017

Just like Bieber, The Weeknd has made a massive fortune over the course of his career.

Involved with Gomez for almost a year, The Weeknd dated her from December 2016 until October 2017. More than just a pop star, The Weeknd is one of those rare performers who comes across as a true artist. For that reason, The Weeknd has sold over 75 million records.

Thanks to his amazing record sales, The Weeknd is worth $300 million.
