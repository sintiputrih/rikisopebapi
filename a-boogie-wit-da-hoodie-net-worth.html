A Boogie wit Da Hoodie: Career

Atlantic Records signed him, and he released an album, “Highbridge the Label: The Takeover Vol.”

His top hits like “My Shit” “Drowning” became recognized among people soon enough.

Not long after,  he performed at BET Awards with other renowned rappers.

By 2017, A Boogie wit Da Hoodie released his first studio album that featured artists like 21 Savage, Trey Songz, and more.

He also achieved nine platinum certifications along with three gold-certified records.
