Nia Jax Reacts To Fans Asking About Opening An OnlyFans

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 1, 0001

Nia Jax was released from her WWE contract, but that won’t be the end of her revenue streams. She will just need to find another way to make some cash.

Jax made it clear that she might not return to the ring. She has her eye on getting involved in other ventures. When the former WWE Superstar recently opened up her Instagram stories to a Q&A, she also realized that some fans want to see a lot more from her.

One fan asked: “Will you start an OnlyFans?” Nia Jax only replied back to say, “Ummm.” That was followed by some eyeballs and a shrugging emoji.

Another fan was a bit more bold. They sent Jax a question to ask: “Have you ever thought about doing porn or opening an OnlyFans.” The Irresistible Force shut that down to make her point very clear. “lol. Absolutely not! Never considered it!”

It seems that Nia Jax’s future plans don’t include getting involved in selling NSFW content. There is nothing wrong with getting into that profession, but it doesn’t seem to be the life for Nia Jax.
