Triple H On WWE/UFC Merger: The Future Is So Bright

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 30, 2024

WWE Chief Content Officer Triple H is excited about working with Endeavor.

In September, WWE and Endeavor closed their transaction agreement, which saw WWE merge with UFC to form TKO. The move kicked off a new era for WWE, and Triple H continues to help lead the company forward into the future.

Speaking with Justin Barrasso of Sports Illustrated, Triple H shared his thoughts on working with Endeavor.

“I can’t tell you how excited I am,” he said. “Working with Endeavor, it’s next level. The flywheel for all the things they touch–if you were trying to build that on your own, it’s taking you a decade. For us, now, it fast tracks everything we’re doing.”

Triple H: Endeavor/UFC Is The Best In The Business

Triple H went on to praise UFC and Endeavor. He stated that the future is bright, and they will take it to a new level.

“Endeavor, UFC, they’re the best in the business at what they do,” he said. “They put things on a different level of professionalism, and we fit right into that. The future is so bright because of the opportunities Endeavor is going to provide for us and the synergies that are there. We’re going to take this to a whole new level.”
