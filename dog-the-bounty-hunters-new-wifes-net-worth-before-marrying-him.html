Dog the Bounty Hunter's beloved wife and business partner, Beth, lost her battle with cancer in June 2019 at the age of 51. It was a devastating loss for Duane. It turns out he was suffering from a pulmonary embolism in the heart due to stress and high blood pressure. Luckily, he has recovered since. "And, of course, it's going to heal." Luckily, Dog found hope in his new love, Francie Frane, a former rancher and hunter who lives in Colorado.
