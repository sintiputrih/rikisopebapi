Where is Fear the Walking Dead Streaming? Find Out Here

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, March 7, 2024

Fear the Walking Dead is a spin-off of the popular zombie drama The Walking Dead, which follows a group of survivors in the early days of the apocalypse. The show has been running since 2015 and is currently in its eighth and final season. But where can you watch Fear the Walking Dead online? Here are the best options for streaming the show.

AMC and AMC+

The most obvious choice for watching Fear the Walking Dead is AMC, the cable network that produces and airs the show. If you have a cable or satellite subscription that includes AMC, you can watch the show live or on-demand on your TV or online via the AMC website or app. However, you will need to sign in with your TV provider credentials to access the episodes.

Alternatively, you can subscribe to AMC+, the streaming service that offers all the content from AMC and its sister channels, such as BBC America, IFC, and Sundance TV. AMC+ costs $8.99 per month and gives you access to all eight seasons of Fear the Walking Dead, as well as other shows like The Walking Dead, The Walking Dead: World Beyond, and The Terror. You can also watch new episodes of Fear the Walking Dead a week before they air on AMC. You can stream AMC+ on various devices, such as Roku, Apple TV, Fire TV, Android TV, and Chromecast. You can also add AMC+ as a channel to other streaming platforms, such as Amazon Prime Video, Apple TV+, and Roku Channel.

Hulu

Another option for streaming Fear the Walking Dead is Hulu, the popular streaming service that offers thousands of movies and shows. Hulu has the rights to the first seven seasons of Fear the Walking Dead, which you can watch with a basic subscription that costs $5.99 per month with ads or $11.99 per month without ads. However, Hulu does not have the rights to the eighth and final season of the show, which is exclusive to AMC and AMC+.
