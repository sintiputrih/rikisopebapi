What climate zone is Nevada in?

Answer

Nevada’s climate zones are classified as 4a through 10a. While the southernmost areas of the state experience a pleasant winter with lows well above freezing, the mountainous regions can experience temperatures as low as -30 degrees Fahrenheit.
