NBA YoungBoy | Career

NBA YoungBoy started making music when he was just fourteen years old. In 2014, he released his first mixtape, “Life Before Fame,” as NBA YoungBoy.

Following that, Gaulden released a series of mixtapes, including ‘Mind of a Menace, Mind of a Menace 2, and Mind of a Menace 3.’

YoungBoy became well-known in 2016 after the release of his mega-popular mixtape, ’38 Baby,’ which received millions of views on YouTube.

YoungBoy’s super hit single “Untouchable” was released in May 2017. On the Billboard Hot 100 list, his song reached number 95. Gaulden then released a video for his song “41.”

His career took off like a rocket after releasing his single “Outside Today” in 2018. The song became his career’s highest-charting tune, debuting at number 35 on the Billboard Hit 100.

YoungBoy’s debut studio album, ‘Until Death Call My Name,’ was released in April 2018.
