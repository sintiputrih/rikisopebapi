HIT ALBUM ALERT: Tom Speight 'Love &amp; Light'

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, October 5, 2024

Embark on a journey through the soul-stirring soundscape of Tom Speight’s third studio album, ‘Love & Light’.

Within the constellation of 12 evocative tracks that comprise the album, Speight masterfully marries the essence of folk and pop influences to create an album full of love, loss, and hope. As each note acts as a conductive force of visceral emotion. 

His lyrical prowess has blossomed from his past two albums, and now delves deeper, resonating brilliantly in anthemic triumphs like ‘Let Go’ and ‘If This Is How The Story Ends’. Talking about the album, Tom notes,

“I’ve touched on things in this record that I’ve never spoken about before; it’s, without doubt, my most personal to date.”

Tom Speight’s artistic journey is one woven with unexpected turns, yet is deserved in every single right, as one of the best upcoming songwriters of this generation. One that will follow in the songwriting halls of Bob Dylan, Paul McCartney and Nick Cave. Confronting life’s challenges and embracing them with unflinching courage. An artist of impeccable lyricism, Speight’s unyielding commitment to pushing the boundaries of his craft is evident and perfected on his latest album, ‘Love & Light’.

An incredible set of tracks that resonates with both fortitude and profound sentiment, Speight underscores an innate gift for fashioning folk-infused pop marvels. With ‘Love & Light’, he beckons his audience to be submerged in his realm new of sound.
