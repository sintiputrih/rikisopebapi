F1NN5TER Career

His first video is called “What F1NN Can Do | WFCD Ep. 1”, uploaded June 3rd, 2015. It’s a two and half minute video about him testing TNT cannons in Minecraft. His most viewed video is “I Became an E-Girl in Minecraft… (bad idea)”, with over 1.2 million views. It’s a stream highlight where he plays Minecraft with a6d while dressing up as Mrs. Claus and going to McDonald’s.

In 2019 he made a video on Hypixel where at one point, he accidentally showed he was using a hacked client, which is a bannable offense on Hypixel. About a week after releasing the video he was permanently banned. Although about another week later he was unbanned with the punishment of losing YouTuber Rank, and not being able to reapply for it until 6 months later.
