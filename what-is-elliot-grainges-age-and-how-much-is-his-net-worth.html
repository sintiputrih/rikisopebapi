He Moved to the United States in 2009 for College

Elliot spent his childhood and teenage years in the U.K. He attended a local private school in the United Kingdom before moving to the United States in 2009. The whole family settled in Los Angeles, while Elliot enrolled in Northeastern University in Boston.

As a student, Elliot started his first business venture with a fellow student partner. After graduating with a bachelor’s degree, he moved back to Los Angeles and launched his record label 10K Project. The label is focused more on artistic integrity than commercial success. The now 30-year-old music executive has successfully made a name for himself away from his father’s shadows.

Who is Elliot Grainge’s Dad, Sir Lucian Charles Grainge

Lucian Charles Grainge has worked in the entertainment industry his entire career. The 63-year-old from North London was once the director of RCA Music Publishing and A&R director for MCA Records before joining Universal Music in 1986. He later rose to become the chairman of the UK division of the Universal Music Group before his promotion to chairman and CEO.

Sir Lucian Charles Grainge has worked with top artists like Lady Gaga, ABBA, Jay-Z, Elton John, The Rolling Stones, Amy Winehouse, Queen, Rihanna, Sam Smith, U2, Katy Perry, and lots more. He reportedly has a net worth of above $50 million and is named the most powerful person in the music industry and the first-ever Executive of the Decade by Billboard magazine. In 2016, he was knighted by Queen Elizabeth for his accomplishments and leadership in the music business. He also has a star to his name on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.

Thanks to his dad, Elliot Grainge had some insight into the workings of the music business before venturing into it. His success in the industry is loosely tied to the experiences he had gathered over the years from watching his dad negotiate deals and sign contracts.
