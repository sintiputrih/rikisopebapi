Jeanette Dube Bio, Blue Frog Ranch Jamie Dube Wife, Age, Job

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, February 29, 2024

Get to know Blue Frog Ranch Jamie Dube’s wife Jeanette Dube. Though Jamie limits his personal life only to his social media, we did some research so that you can know more about his wife in this article.

From age to job, to her family members, all the details unfold as this article proceeds.

Blue Frog Ranch: Meet Jeanette Dube, Jamie Dube Wife

Jamie Dube, the star of Blue Frog Ranch is currently married to his wife Jeanette Dube. Although they never divulged information on how long they have been married to each other, it is likely that they exchanged wedding vows a couple of decades before.

Throughout the years of their time together, the married couple has been very supportive of each other. In January 2022, Jeanette proudly shared of her husband making it in as the cast member of Blind Frog Ranch.

Jeanette proclaimed on her Facebook, “So proud of my husband, Jamie Dube, the newest cast member of “Mystery at Blind Frog Ranch.” The season premiere is tonight at 10 pm on Discovery Channel. Jamie – you have worked so hard for this and made many sacrifices along the way. I never doubted for a minute that you would achieve your goal. It’s just who you are!! I am so incredibly proud of you!”

But, Jamie’s success isn’t only his and his wife’s to share. The happily married couple are parents to two children; a son named Brady Dube and a daughter named Bryanna Dube.

On National Son’s Day Jeanette took to her Facebook and posted, “On National Son’s Day… Brady, I am so proud of you! You bring me so much joy and I love you so much!! Thanks for being you!!” Jamie seconded his wife’s sentiment in the post by adding in the comment section, “Brady I must say you raised the bar. I couldn’t be more proud of you ♡ .”

Brady is currently based in Orrington, Maine, and has graduated from high school.

As for their daughter, the oldest of the two, Bryanna worked at Coach House Restaurant. She attended the University of Maine at Orono.

Talking about her husband, Jamie turned 54 years old in May 2022. He was born in Howland, Maine, and has a twin sister Julie Casburn and a brother Mark Dube.

Proud of his Discovery stint, Jamie’s twin had also taken to her Facebook to appreciate him “I am so proud of my twin!!!! Jamie started his journey many years ago and look at him now…he’s on The Discovery Channel. Check him out tonight at 10:00 p.m. on Mystery at Blind Frog Ranch. Again, so so proud of you Jamie! ”

Meanwhile, his brother has been a jeweler and working in the sales of Diamond & Gold Mine Outlet & Local Coupon Outlet for the past 18 years. So, it’s true that he and Jamie had collaborated in a jewelry business that they called Mark Jamie Dube.

Jamie Dube joins Duane, his son Chad, who is the head of ranch security Charlie Snider, and world-renowned geologist Eric Drummond, in their quest for Aztec gold.

Like everyone on the team, Jamie is also convinced that other than the Lost Rhodes Mines Gold there is some big paranormal activity hidden inside the caverns of the Utah Mountains.

(Of course) this is not the first time Jamie is out hunting and investigating the paranormal.

A former police officer-turned-real estate agent, he founded East Coast Ghost Trackers sometime ago. East Coast, if you should know, is a group that has led guided ghost tours at Fort Knox (a Maine resource guide company) for years.

Confident there will be spirits at night, he and Ken Ort had founded the company.

Around that time, Jamie also published a book, “Where Do You Walk?” penned about getting into ghost-hunting, entities at Fort Knox, and some of the families he said the team has helped. One could also find the book on Amazon.

Jeanette Dube Age

In February 2023, Jeanette Dube turned 49 years old. So, her birth year is 1974.

What Is Jeanette Dube Maiden Name?

Jeanette Dube’s maiden name is Abbott. She is the daughter of Robert James Abbott Sr. Robert Sr. was born to James R. and Verna Mae (Kennedy) Abbott on 26 April 1951 in Phillips. He was educated in the schools of Phillips. Robert worked for several years at Farmington Shoe Co. & Franklin Shoe Co. in Farmington. As a young man, he also worked at Forster Mfg. Co. in Strong. Robert enjoyed reading and was an avid movie buff.

Besides Jeanette, Robert Sr. also had a son named Robert J. Abbott Jr. and his wife Lori of Arizona. Besides Bryanna and Brady, he was also a grandfather to Katelyn, Joshua, and Eric.

Robert died on 18 August 2010 at his home on High St. in Farmington at the age of 59.

Jeanette Dube Job

Jeanette Dube, according to her Facebook, is working as an account manager at Northern Light Health. She was previously employed at Affiliated Healthcare Systems.

Regarding her education, Jeanette completed her high school at Mt. Abram High School. She attended college at the University of Maine.
