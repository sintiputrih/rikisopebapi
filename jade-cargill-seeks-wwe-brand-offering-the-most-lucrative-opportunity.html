Jade Cargill Seeks WWE Brand Offering the Most Lucrative Opportunity

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, June 21, 2024

The Storm of WWE, Jade Cargill arrived in the global juggernaut as one of the top future prospects for the women’s division. Despite being in the company for months, Jade has yet to select a brand for her showcase.

However, she recently opened up about her thoughts on signing with WWE’s main roster shows, RAW & SmackDown. In recent interview with Denise Salcedo during the press event following the WrestleMania 40 Kickoff event, Jade Cargill opened up about potentially signing with either WWE brand.

Cargill revealed that she had two prospects to analyze regarding her main roster signing. The first would be which brand offers her a lucrative deal and the second would be the enthralling matches against top WWE stars they would put her against.

“I love Raw, it’s a long night, but I love me some SmackDown. I don’t know yet. I don’t know, I want to see who can give me the biggest bid and who can promise me some great matches.”

Jade Cargill has already impressed the audience with her debut performance in the 2024 Women’s Royal Rumble match, despite not winning the encounter.

With the road to WrestleMania 40 in full force, it could be interesting to see the former AEW TBS champion included in the cards for the Grandest Stage of them all.
