Professional Life and Career of Daniela Aedo

Aedo began her professional acting career at the age of three, appearing as ‘Isabel’ in the 1998 film “Vivo por Elena.” Because of her tiny role, she did not receive much attention.

In 2000, at the age of 5, she participated in the T.V series “Carita de ngel” as ‘Dulce Mara Larios Valle,’ for which she received widespread acclaim and favourable feedback from fans and viewers. For the same film, she also received a “Premios INTE Award” in the area of “Child Talent.”

In 2002, she participated in the television series “Vivan Los Nios!” for which she received another “Premios INTE Award” in the category of “Child Talent.” She debuted in the television series “Mujer, Casos de la Vida Real” in 2003 and participated in a total of three episodes till 2005.

Other films in which she has appeared include “Mi Pecado” (2009), “La Familia P. Luche” (2006), “Plaza Sésamo” (2005), and others.
