It’s the $50 white T-shirt you’re convinced will change your life, only to learn it too gets pit stains and turns “vintage” white after just a few months of wear. I’ve been there enough times to have learned my lesson, which is why when I want to refresh my wardrobe basics and loungewear staples, I go immediately to Hanes.
