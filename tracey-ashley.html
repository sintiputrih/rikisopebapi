Tracey Ashley (Comedian) Wiki, Age, Husband, Biography, Family Facts

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 28, 2024

Tracey Ashley (born July 08, 1968) is an American Actress, Writer, Stand Up Comedian, and Social Media Personality from Miami, Florida. Recently, it was announced by Netflix in early August that Tracey will star in the upcoming show.

On August 13, 2019, Tracey made a debut on the show called “Tiffany Haddish Presents: They Ready“. The program mainly features Tiffany Haddish (actress, comedian, singer, model, and author) in the main lead.

Furthermore, she works with six of her favorite comedians in the series. The other main casts of the “They Ready” show Flame Monroe, April Macie, Aida Rodriguez, Chaunté Wayans, and Marlo Williams.

In fact, each of the artists has their own half-hour stand-up special on Netflix program. Haddish also serves as an executive producer alongside Wanda Sykes (TV personality, comedian, and writer) and Page Hurwitz (producer).

Career, Shows List & Net worth

The 51-years-old comedian initiated her profession as a cruise director. She used to manage a staff of 25 to 50 people while overseeing onboard activities. According to a media outlet, Tracey worked on the Dolphin Cruise Lines’ Seabreeze.

Ashley then thought of pursuing her passion as she did various comedy skits at her college feast. Moreover, she received her first nomination as a Best Female Performer by Campus Activities Magazine.

The actress also became one of the semi-finalists on NBC’s “Last Comic Standing”. Later, she began to work as a co-host on TV Land’s Prime Movies. Her breakthrough turning point in Hollywood came when she starred “Wanda Sykes Presents: Herlarious“.

The hardworking and talented Tracey Ashley’s Net worth is $1.3 million US Dollars. In 2016, the artist also appeared as Karen on The Don’t We Boys: Sketch Corp.
