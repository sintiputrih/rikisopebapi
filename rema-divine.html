Rema Divine

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 5, 2024

Raving Nigerian recording artiste, Rema is here with a beautiful song labeled; “Divine” off his just released studio album; “Rave & Roses”.

This latest project is coming after three years of Rema in the music industry with several smash hit songs, beautiful videos and outstanding EP’s. ‘Rave & Roses’ happens to be his first even studio album.

The star-studded studio album houses sixteen exceptional tracks and features American superstar, Chris Brown, rapper 6Lack, British rapper, AJ Tracey and singer, Yseult. According to Rema; ‘Here’s a piece of my soul to you‘.

Before the body of work arrived, the Mavin Records star had already released ‘Calm Down’, ‘FYN (Fresh Young Nigga)’ and ‘Soundgasm’ which were all taken form the just released maiden studio album.

ALSO: Shexy Ayoz ft Blaqbonez – Jojo

Rema, originally called Divine Ikubor has been in the music scene for a while and has been consistent throughout. He gain prominence with the release of his project; “Iron Man”, a song which appeared on Barack Obama’s 2019 summer playlist.

Rema joined Jonzing World Records, a subsidiary of Mavin Records in 2019. He’s well known for his songs; Dumebi and Ginger. Rema was nominated for the best new act at the BET Awards in 2020. He loves doing music and is doing well in the quest for a successful musical career.

‘Divine’ is to whet the appetite for more music. Take a listen below and don’t forget to share your thoughts with us !
