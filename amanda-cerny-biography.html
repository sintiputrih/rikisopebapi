Amanda Cerny Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, January 17, 2024

Who is Amanda Cerny?

Amanda Cerny is a famous YouTuber and Social Media Influencer. She has a massive fan-following on the vine and subscribers to her YouTube channel. She was a cover model for Health & Wellness magazine.

Apart from it, she is also a comedian, actress, and director. Her comedy sketches often collaborate with King Bach and Logan Paul.

Who are the parents of Amanda Cerny?

Amanda Cerny was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania U.S.A on June 26, 1991. There is no information about her mother but her father’s name is Russell Allen Cerny.

She has one sister and one half-brother Samantha and Nick Bateman respectively.

Amanda Cerny always keeps her brother and sister along with her in social media. Amanda holds American citizenship and Caucasian ethnicity. She was raised in Florida and later migrated to Los Angeles with Samantha.

Till she was of eight years she moved to five different states along with her family.

Education, School/College University

Apart from studying, Amanda Cerny also received a black belt in karate by the age of eleven and enrolled in her sporting efforts towards the track team in high school.

Being studious and getting high test scores in high school, Amanda Cerny got a seat to study at Florida State University and studied International Affairs.

Cerny was very much interested in the field of modeling that soon after completing her graduation she sent a packet of her photos at. Playboy. She was also very active in sports. She dropped out of the university after getting an invitation from the Mansion for the test shot on L.A.

Amanda Cerny: Professional Life, and Career

Amanda Cerny is forward in her profession holding multiple talents of being an actress, director, and former model. At a shoot in Playboy Playmates, she shot a multifaceted personality shot. She is also the cover model for Health and Wellness magazine.

Keeping it as a fun hobby, Amanda joined the stage of modeling. Later she got focused on it after her classmate was highlighted in Playboy magazine. Fortunately, she was called by the magazine and was featured in many of its editions.

Later Amanda Cerny shifted to social media uploading funny vines on YouTube, Snapchat, Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook. Making great videos led her successfully to millions of followers increasing day by day. Amanda came into the limelight in the Vine Community along with her best friend and fellow social media inspiration Lele Pons.

Apart from it she was also a successful actress and performed in various movies. Some of these include “The Coach Who Clothed Them”, “Internet Famous” and “The Bet” in 2016. Her role is The Bet as Mrs. McDoogle was very much appreciated by her fans.

In 2017 Amanda Cerny got many chances to show her talent on screen. She worked in Deported, Public Disturbance, and Superstition: The Rule of 3’s and Airplane Mode.

Awards, Nominations
