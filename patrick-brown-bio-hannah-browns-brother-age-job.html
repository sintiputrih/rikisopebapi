Who Is Patrick Brown, Hannah Brown's Brother?+Bio, Age, Job, Instagram

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Hannah Brown put quite a show in “The Bachelorette.” One of Hannah’s ex’s ex started a relationship with her brother Patrick Brown. Jed Wyatt, who Hannah dated in the reality show, previously dated a singer named Hayley Stevens. Now Hayley and Patrick are dating each other and the relationship was confirmed in February 2021.

Let’s learn more about Patrick and his current relationship with this Patrick Brown Bio. Here we also cover his age and job.

Meet Patrick Brown, Hannah Brown’s Brother

Patrick Brown is best known as the younger brother of Hannah Brown. They are two children born to Robert Brown and Susanne Brown. Throughout her pageantry career and career in The Bachelorette, Patrick and his parents have been her greatest cheerleader. Patrick is two years younger than Hannah and shares a strong and deep bond.

Patrick often shared pictures of her sister when she competed in The Bachelorette. He was very proud of his sister throughout her quest to find love on reality shows. The most recent picture that he uploaded of his sister was back in June 2020. He wrote, “Gonna miss you HB! Always will be my favorite Beast! See you soon sis!”

In March 2020, Patrick made a headline after overdosing. He revealed that he spent two days on a ventilator because of that. He wrote a lengthy post on 17 March 2020 about his experience after the overdose.

Patrick considered it the second chance at life and he felt so much closer to god.

While speaking with US Weekly, Hannah also addressed the changes that he made in his life after the 2020 overdose. Hannah shared “I love my brother and I’m just so thankful for all the changes that he’s made in his life.” She added further, “Because that just really impacted the way that our family dynamic is. Both of us have kind of gone on our journey of healing.”

Hannah shared that she was very proud of all the things Patrick did to make his life better.

Patrick Brown Dating Hannah Brown’s Ex’s Ex?

Patrick Brown is dating his sister ex’s ex. Yes, Patrick is in a relationship with Jed Wyatt’s ex-girlfriend Haley Stevens. It was Haley who confirmed the relationship with an IG post in February 2021. She wrote, “Here’s to laughing ‘til our bellies hurt, my happiest birthday, and You. ❤️”

Meanwhile, Patrick reposted the snap on his own IG story. The relationship took everyone by surprise after they were spotted together in each other’s socials. As already revealed, after Jed joined the show, a rumor spread that he already had a girlfriend and it was none other than Hayley.

Hayley revealed that he had spoken to her about joining the show to promote his music career. He had promised that he would return once he was done with the show. Hayley revealed to US Weekly, “As a fellow musician and as somebody that just loves this person, I’m like, ‘Why would I hold him back from this opportunity?’ I know the premise of the show. Even though that’s not an ideal situation for our current relationship, when you’re not in it, it seems like a movie.”

Hannah broke her silence on her brother dating Hayley. She exclusively told US Weekly that she doesn’t choose who Patrick loves and who he wants to be with. Nevertheless, she wants him to be happy and she loves him no matter what.

Patrick Brown Age

As of November 2021, Patrick Brown was 24 years old.

Patrick Brown Job

Talking about the job, Patrick is the owner of ProServe Heating and Air Conditioning company. He founded the company in December 2018 and has been providing service while based in Birmingham, Alabama ever since.

According to the company’s website, it lets the customer bypass the outrageous pricing and the horrible customer service experience to receive the best quality product and service at a reasonable price.

The services that his company provides include new installation with a customized HVAC package that fits the client’s budget. The full HVAC replacement service starts at $3,900. They also provide a repair service with an additional $85 diagnostic fee. They also brought a maintenance plan that cost $99.
