How Old Is Wayne Mahar? Everything To Know As The Meteorologist Announces Retirement

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Wayne Mahar, aged 65, is a chief meteorologist at Central New York Community and is retiring from the station after more than thirty-five years of association.

Wayne Mahar is one of the oldest and renowned meteorologists who specializes in severe weather preparation for outdoor events and concerts.

He has organized and spoken at many seminars internationally on severe weather safety. He has also provided lectures, panel discussions, and Table Top exercises at numerous international events to discuss how better to deal with the next SANDY.

He is one of the first television meteorologists who committed to broadcasting the outdoor weather events in the Central New York Community.

Wayne Mahar Retires At Age 65- Wikipedia Bio

Wayne Mahar, aged 65, announced his retirement as the chief meteorologist after working for more than 35 years at CNY Central Television Stations, according to syracuse.com.

He joined the station in Syracuse, New York, in January 1985 and has built an impressive career in weather forecasting and television broadcasting.

Mahar made his retirement announcement during a live broadcast. He confirmed that he will be retiring by December.

Further, he established Precision Weather Service in 1972, as per his LinkedIn account.

It is a private weather consulting firm that provides services worldwide. It also deals in forensic meteorology and researching past weather events.

Mahar is also one of four inductees to the New York State Broadcasters Hall of Fame 2021. He will be inducted in October for his contribution to weather forecasting and television broadcasting.

He is a member of the American Meteorological Society and Canadian Meteorological and Oceanographic Society.

Who Is Wayne Mahar Married To? Wife Name Revealed

Wayne Mahar’s wife is Dianne. The couple has been married for a long time and is the parents of two adult daughters, Emily and Caitlin.

Moreover, he loves spending time with his family and traveling with them outside of his work. He grew up in Brunswick, Maine.

He is an avid hockey fan and supports teams like the New Jersey Devils, Boston Bruins, and Buffalo Sabres.
