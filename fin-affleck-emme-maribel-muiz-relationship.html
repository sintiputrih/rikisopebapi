Unfortunately for some families that were formed the way Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck’s clan came together, step-siblings often don’t get along. As a result, Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck may have been nervous about how their teenage children would get along.

Thankfully for everyone involved, it seems very clear that at the very least, Ben Affleck’s middle child and one of Jennifer Lopez’s twins get along extremely well. After all, Fin Affleck and Emme Muñiz seem to have an extremely close bond.

Of course, Fin Affleck and Emme Muñiz aren’t famous movie stars like Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck are. As a result, it makes perfect sense that Emme Muñiz and Fin Affleck don’t go around granting interviews.
