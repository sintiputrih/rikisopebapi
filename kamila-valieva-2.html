Kamila Valieva also known as Kamila Valeryevna Valieva, is a Russian figure skater, dancer, entrepreneur and social media Personality. As of 2022, Kamila Valieva’s net worth is $2 million.

Valieva is the current world record holder for the women’s short program, free skating, and total scores. She has set nine world records during her career.
