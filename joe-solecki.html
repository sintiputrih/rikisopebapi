Joe Solecki Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Married, Facts, Career

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 30, 2024

Table of Biography

Joe Solecki is an American mixed martial artist (MMA) fighter. Joe Solecki competes in the lightweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). Since turning pro in 2016, he has played in the CFFC, Warfare, 864 Fighting Championship, and Ring of Combat (ROC).

Early Life

Joe Solecki is a thirty-year-old actor who was born on September 27, 1993. He has an American nationality.

Professional Life

He stands 5’9″ (175.26 CM) tall and weighs 155 LBS (70.31 KG) in terms of his physique. Since he was six years old, he has been training in mixed martial arts. Joe made his professional debut against Chris Rollins at CFFC 61 – Anyanwu vs. Pinto on October 29, 2016. He was able to submit to his opponent and win the fight. At CFFC 62- Santella vs. Lozano, he faced Andrew Chirico. He was able to submit to his opponent and win the fight.

On the 18th of February 2017, he submitted Kevin Perez CFFC 63. He lost the first match of his career against Cesar Balmaceda on May 20, 2017, at CCFC 65 – Brady vs. Saraceno. He bounced back from the loss by facing Eric Calderson at Warefare FC. The fight ended in a TKO victory for him. On the 16th of December 2017, he returned to the ring at CFFC 96, where he won by submission.

Joe was knocked out by Nikolas Motta in round three of the 864 Fighting Championship on June 21, 2018. After that, he defeated Gilbert Patrocinio and Jacob Bohn at ROC 66 and CFFC 73, respectively.

Joe beat James Wallace in the third episode of Dana White’s Contender Series – Season 3 on June 9, 2019.

He made his UFC debut against Matt Wiman on December 7th, 2019 at UFC on ESPN 7 – Overseem vs. Rozenstruik. Joe won the fight by a majority verdict. In his next fight, Munhoz vs. Edgar, he defeated Austin Hubbard by submission at UFC on ESPN 15 – Munhoz vs. Edgar. He defeated Jim Miller at UFC on ABC 2 – Vettori vs. Holland on April 10, 2021.
