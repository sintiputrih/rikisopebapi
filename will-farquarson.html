Will Farquarson Biography

Will Farquarson is best known as a Bassist, Guitarist who has an estimated Net Worth of $5 Million. Though he is best known for his work with the English rock band Bastille, this guitarist, bassist, and percussionist also collaborated with artists and bands such as Ella Eyre, Rudimental, and Naughty Boy. He was born on September 22, 1983, in England. He has earned over 50,000 followers on Twitter. Bad Blood, his first album with Bastille, was released in 2013. He became bandmates with Bastille frontman Dan Smith. He was raised by hippie parents and, during his childhood years in England, he lived on a boat. His zodiac sign is Virgo.
