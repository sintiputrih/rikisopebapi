James Middleton &amp; Fiance Alizee Thevenet Make Rare Appearance In London

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, January 30, 2024

James Middleton is all smiles next to fiancée Alizee Thevenet while attending the Bvlgari Magnifica Gala dinner held at Spencer House on Thursday evening (June 24) in London.

The younger brother of Kate Middleton and Pippa Middleton, James and Alizee dressed up nice for the event.

James chose a velvet suit jacket while Alizee opted for a pretty coral floral dress.

PHOTOS: Check out the latest pics of James Middleton

The recently engaged couple had to put their wedding on hold amid the pandemic.

“It’s very sad, but it’s just not practical to hold it in this crisis,” one of his friends shared last year about the postponement.

However, the two are still planning to tie the knot soon.

“It’s a year since I asked Alizee to marry me ? … what a year it’s been!” James previously wrote on Instagram. “Two homes ? lockdown ? two attempts at a wedding??❌, a litter of ?, launching of new company @ella.co ? a beard shave ? and much more but the best part is that I get to share it with you @alizeethevenet and I can’t wait to take on whatever the future holds for us ✨.”
