Michael Wirth is an American businessman born and raised Golden, Colorado, USA. Wirth is currently the CEO of Chevron. He started the position in 2017 after succeeding John S. Watson. Prior to being the CEO, he was previously served as the vice-chairman of the board of directors of the company.

    Wirth joined Chevron in 1982. He has worked in multiple sectors including engineering, construction, and operations. In 2001, Wirth became the president of marketing for Chevron’s Asia, Africa, and the Middle East region.
