Cecily Strong Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, April 4, 2024

Bio:
Cecily Strong (born February 8, 1984 in Springfield, Illinois) is an American actress, voice actress, and comedian.

Best Known For:
Cecily is best known for being a cast member of Saturday Night Live. She joined the show in 2012. Cecily also made headlines as the featured entertainer at the 2015 White House Correspondents’ Association dinner.

Personal Life:
Cecily dated former SNL cast member Mike O’Brien in 2014, but the two have not been seen together much in 2015, leading many to believe they may have split.
