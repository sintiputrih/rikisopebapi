Meet Alessandra Gonzalez, the Daughter of Adrian Gonzalez

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, January 24, 2024

Adrian Gonzalez is a retired Mexican-American professional baseball first baseman who played in the Major League Baseball (MLB). Over the course of his 14 seasons career, he played for the Texas Rangers, San Diego Padres, Boston Red Sox, Los Angeles Dodgers, and New York Mets before going on retirement from the beautiful game.

Gonzalez played high school baseball for the Eastlake High School in Chula Vista, California before he was drafted first overall pick by the Florida Marlins in the 2000. He received a signing on bonus of $3 million dollars. After three seasons, which was cut short by a wrist injury, Gonzalez was traded to the Texas Rangers where he made his MLB debut in 2004.
