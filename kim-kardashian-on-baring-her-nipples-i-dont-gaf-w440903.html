Kim Kardashian on Baring Her Nipples: I Dont GAF'

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Free the nipple! Kim Kardashian couldn’t care less about what people think of her sexy, sheer outfits. The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 35, shared her favorite go-to items in a new app post titled “Sheer Vibes” on Monday, September 19.

“I have always loved sheer,” the mom of two wrote. “I just don’t GAF, LOL.”

For Kardashian, the more see-through, the better. Some of her favorite pieces include a geo lace bralette from American Apparel ($32), a black Urban Outfitters mesh long-sleeved top ($29), a NaaNaa sheer long-sleeved mini dress ($52) and a Charlotte Russe double strap mesh bralette that retails at just $11.

The Selfish author likes to splurge, too, of course. Some of the most expensive items in her closet are La Perla timeless frame underwire bras that go for $264 each.

Kardashian has shown off her tiny figure in sheer ensembles several times this past month. Over the weekend, she bared her breasts in a nude bodysuit while out in Miami on September 17. That same day, she slipped into a low-cut sheer black dress while attending husband Kanye West’s concert. 

Kardashian has always been vocal about body empowerment. Back in March, she wrote a blog post about daring to bare after stars such as Bette Midler and Chloë Grace Moretz slammed her for posting nude selfies. 

“I am empowered by my sexuality. I am empowered by feeling comfortable in my skin. I am empowered by showing the world my flaws and not being afraid of what anyone is going to say about me. And I hope that through this platform I have been given, I can encourage the same empowerment for girls and women all over the world,” she wrote at the time. 

She added: “The body-shaming and slut-shaming—it’s like, enough is enough. I will not live my life dictated by the issues you have with my sexuality. You be you and let me be me. I am a mother. I am a wife, a sister, a daughter, an entrepreneur and I am allowed to be sexy.”
