Joey Kramer’s net worth is $100 million


Joey Kramer: A Brief Biography

Born on June 21, 1950, in The Bronx, New York City, Joseph Michael Kramer is a renowned musician recognized for his role as the drummer in the esteemed hard rock band Aerosmith. Kramer has been an integral part of the band since its formation in 1970, effectively defining its rhythm section and contributing significantly to its success.

Joey Kramer started off with humble beginnings and through dedication and passion, he has amassed a net worth that stands at an impressive $100 million as of late 2016, primarily through his career in music.

Joey Kramer’s Financial Status: $100 Million

Joey’s journey started when, at the age of 14, he found his inspiration in The Beatles and decided to pursue a career in music. His early days involved playing for a variety of bands, including The Dynamics, The Medallions, King Bees, and Strawberry Ripple. His formal introduction to Aerosmith occurred in 1970 when he joined the band and signed with Columbia Records. Their albums “Toys in the Attic” and “Rocks” brought them global recognition, solidifying their positions in the music industry and contributing significantly to Kramer’s wealth.

Over the years, Aerosmith’s career witnessed ups and downs, but they managed to make a striking comeback with the release of “Permanent Vacation” in 1987, followed by the blockbuster success of “Get a Grip” in 1993. The band’s widespread fame was further propelled by their music videos which garnered numerous awards, and their song “I Don’t Want to Miss a Thing” further solidified their place in the music industry.

Although the band faced challenges, such as Kramer’s health issues during the “Let Rock Rule” tour in 2014, they have persevered, with Joey returning to the group after a brief hiatus. His personal life also reflects his strength, having been married twice and being a dedicated father to his two children.
