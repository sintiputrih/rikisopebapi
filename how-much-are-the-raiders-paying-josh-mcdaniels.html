How much are the raiders paying Josh McDaniels?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, March 24, 2024

The Las Vegas Raiders have made the decision to part ways with head coach Josh McDaniels, just 25 games into his two-year contract. Despite a poor record of 9-16, McDaniels' contract was worth a staggering $10 million per year, making him one of the highest-paid coaches in the NFL. The firing will cost the Raiders an estimated $60 million, as McDaniels is owed the remaining four years of his contract. While the team may consider buying him out, it is unlikely that McDaniels would accept any offer from the Raiders.

This move comes just two seasons after McDaniels signed a six-year contract with the team, which was reportedly paying him $10 million annually. With his departure, the Raiders will still have to fulfill the financial obligations of the remaining four years on his contract. McDaniels' net worth is estimated to be around $6 million, further highlighting the significant financial commitment the Raiders made in hiring him.

The decision to let go of McDaniels has not been well received by the Raider Nation, with some fans expressing skepticism and disappointment. Despite the hefty investment in McDaniels, the team's lackluster performance and inability to meet expectations ultimately led to his dismissal.

As for McDaniels' future in the NFL, it remains uncertain. While he has shown promise as an offensive coordinator, his tenure as a head coach has been underwhelming. It is unclear if any teams will be willing to take a chance on him in the future, given his recent track record.
