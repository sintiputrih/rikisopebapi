Dave Kindig is a TV star famous for his show, “Bitching Rides.” Growing up Kindig had a passion for Hot Wheels, drawing and Legos and it’s no wonder he’s now made a career out of it. He is very successful as the owner and chief designer of the custom car shop ‘Kindig-It’. The store is located in Salt Lake, Utah and has 27,000 square feet of work space.

He with his entire team of skilled mechanics and designers redesigned several cars. They start by sketching out his ideas, then they carve out the model on paper, and finally they put his idea into practice. His service is expensive and Dave realizes it’s not affordable for everyone. But still, due to his popularity status, he has clients lining up to receive his opinion. Now let’s learn more about Dave.

Dave Kindig: early life and education

Dave Rick Kindig was born on February 6, 1991 in Salt Lake, Utah. As it happens with people with normal people before they find fame, not much is known about his early life. Although Dave, in his teens, has a deep passion for cars and creativity.

At age 17, Dave fell in love with classic Volkswagen Beetles. This creative individual then started working as a graphic and customization designer. There have also been reports of him working for 8 long years in the field of high performance coatings company. Those companies worked with ceramic coating on the exhaust system to display the cars at car shows.

Dave Kindig: Career

Dave likes to constantly joke that he has an engineering degree in Hot Wheels and Legos. He, however, is a self-made designer who never went to art school. He hasn’t even attended college, but got into this field because of his love of cars. Dave was passionate about drawing and designing model cars, so he put together lego pieces to design cars.

Most recently, Dave has his own television show, ‘Bitching Rides’, and is among the most watched shows on the Velocity network. The show premiered in 2014, but long before that, it was a prominent name in the design industry. This program just added shine to his work and gave him the marketing power, and now the business is booming.

Kindig offers a unique service and is equally expensive. He, on average, earns a six figure sum to redesign a car and that is not something that many can afford. He entered this field at the age of 29, hoping to showcase his creative talents and it is exactly what he has been able to do today. Interestingly, he never uses computers to create designs, instead he draws them in about 2 minutes. Today Dave has been able to extend his service to big names in the industry including Lotus, Lamborghini, Dodge and Harley Davidson.

Dave Kindig: Personal Life

Dave is married to his beloved Charity Kindig. The two have been together since 1992 and his wife is the co-founder of Kindig-it Design. They have embarked on a car restoration service journey in their manufacturing workshop.

The family grew in size when Charity gave birth to her first child Baylee and later a son Drew. In 2016, Dave’s daughter Baylee got engaged and Dave announced the news on Twitter.

Dave constantly mentions his wife as the best thing that ever happened to him. He was inspired by Charity to follow her dreams of owning a custom car shop. The duo have now celebrated 26 years of being together. They seem happy and perfect for each other and we wish them all the luck in the future.

Dave Kindig: History

Kindig is the owner of ‘Kindig-It Design’ and has been with the company for decades. His factory covers an area of ​​27,00 square feet and has personal, commercial and design buildings. He earns most of his money from modifying cars and bicycles, and his participation in the TV show also has advantages for him. The Bitchin’ Rides program has been a huge success over the years and continues to make money from merchandise sales.

As of 2018, his net worth is in the range of $3 million. She has been a hugely successful name in the industry, but remains the same humble and loving person. Kindig is also active on social media and has over 208,000 followers on Instagram .
